What are human rights?

Human rights are the basic rights and freedoms that belong to every person in the world, from birth until death. They apply regardless of where you are from, what you believe or how you choose to live your life.

How did it impact our world?

Human rights connect us to each other through a shared set of rights and responsibilities. A person's ability to enjoy their human rights depends on other people respecting those rights.
